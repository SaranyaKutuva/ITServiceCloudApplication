About Our Services

BIT field support services is a division of Business Information Technology Pty Ltd (BIT).We provide IT support services. Our IT professionals are available as consultants for your business. You might want some advice on how to set up your networks or information on which machines and software would be most appropriate for your business. We are happy to work with you closely to make sure you are getting the technology and support that your business needs to succeed.

Business IT Support includes:

  • Installing, configuring & troubleshooting PC
  • Software and hardware installation
  • On-site and remote support
  • Periodic IT audits
  • Data Security, protection and back up
  • Desktop, laptop, tablet, printer, phone support, maintenance and monitoring
  • Wi-fi, internet setup, troubleshooting & fixing slow connections
  • Networking issue resolution for LAN and WAN

Why BIT Services

  • EXPERT MANANGEMENT: An understanding of IT issues and their solutions is key to timely resolution. Our experience and experts provide this
  • COST MANANGEMENT: Managing IT service and reducing IT operation cost is the goal of every business and our services facilitate this for our customers
  • ONSITE SUPPORT IN ADDITION TO REMOTE SUPPORT: Remote support is fantastic and sometimes it is handy to have someone onsite to fix the issues. We maintain this standard for our customers constantly
  • GUARANTEED FIX: We act proactively ensure guaranteed service to our customers. Backup, redundancy and workaround are our key elements of providing guaranteed services
  • OUR SUPPORT VISIBILITY/SYSTEM: We use an interactive cloud based ticketing system which is easy to use, log faults, monitor service tasks and easy to follow up. With this system our service desk can be integrated with your business needs.
  • ON CALL SUPPORT: Some of our customers only need infrequent IT support. They simply call us and we provide remote remote or onsite support as required
  • PROJECT: Our projects include small to large setups including office setup, network setup, new server/rack, application, hardware setup, office relocation and anything related to IT
